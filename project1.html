Revival ｜Shelley Collins’ Vintage Art Journey

Documentary, Audiovisual

2024

This short documentary explores Shelley Collins’ passion for collecting and preserving vintage art posters. The film follows her behind the scenes as she curates and installs a new exhibition at her Melbourne studio, blending nostalgia with visual storytelling.

Subject

  • The artistic journey of turning a personal passion into a successful business.
  • The main character’s opinions on the value and historical significance of restoring vintage posters.
  • The vintage art and cultural community in Melbourne.

Story Character

Shelley Collins, originally a passionate collector of vintage posters during her time in the U.S., decided to turn this passion into a business upon her return to Melbourne.

Story

The Galerie founded by Shelley started as an online gallery and eventually evolved into a physical store that has become a part of Melbourne’s art community. Throughout the documentary, Shelley’s journey from a collector to an entrepreneur will be explored in depth, showcasing how she has managed to preserve her love for art while successfully navigating the world of business. The narrative will explore her opinions, the challenges she has faced, such as sourcing vintage posters, building a customer group, and so on.
